Bio

Eduardo has over 12 years of graphic design experience with a focus on photo editing, vector and motion graphics. The depth of his experience covers a wide area of responsibilities within the industry. He is a proficient user of the Adobe Creative Suites; extensive experience in Final Cut, Cinema 4D, iMovie, HTML5, CSS, Bootstrap, Git and working knowledge of JavaScript and Java. He is bilingual in Spanish and English. Most recently, he has gained invaluable experience as a Multimedia Producer at one of the top 37 local Fox affiliates in the United States. His positions at Fox Carolina and Lucky XP Marketing and Promotions have afforded him the opportunity of complex collaboration across multiple departments, managing a wide variety of projects simultaneously, and delivering a finished product that is on-brand, on-time and consistently exceeds expectations. As an ambitious and dedicated person, he is capable of working efficiently under any deadline while maintaining creative problem-solving skills and a broad visual language and reach.
